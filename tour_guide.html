Chittagong/city of ports


Chittagong is the biggest seaport and second largest town in Bangladesh situated near the Bay of Bengal. Famous for hill areas, natural beauty and for the seashore. Chittagong is also known of the town of Aulias (Muslim saints).
Its green hills and forests, its broad sandy beaches and its fine cool climate always attract the holiday-markers. Described by the Chinese traveler poet, Huen Tsang (7th century A.D) as “a sleeping beauty emerging from mists. It combines the busy hum of an active seaport with the shooting quiet of a charming hill town.



Major Tourist Spots in Chittagong Division :


Patenga and Fouzdarhat Sea Beaches

Patenga & Fouzdarhat sea beach are the major attractions of Chittagong for the tourists. Besides the sea beach, you can enjoy boating and river cruise in the River Karnaphuli. You can see the busy seaport activities here.


Shrine of Hazrat Bayezead Bostami

One can visit the ancient rare variety of Turtles in the Dighi in the Shrine premises. This shrine attracts a large number of visitors and pilgrims. At its base is a large tank with several hundred tortoises.


Sitakundu

The famous Chandranath Temple & Buddhist temples are in Sitakundu, 37 km for from Chittagong city.

Shylet/City of Tea


This Occupies the north east part of Bangladesh. It is a natural hilly, forest area with ox bow lakes and famous shrines which is known as the tea capital of Bangladesh, is the actual tea center of the area.
Nestled in the picturesque Surma Valley amidst scenic tea plantations and lush green tropical forests, greater Sylhet is a prime attraction for all tourists visiting Bangladesh. The valley has good number of haors, which are big natural depressions. Sylhet is a district of saints, shrines and daring but virile people.



Major Tourist Spots in Shylet Division :


Madhabkunda Waterfall

The journey to Madhabkunda is exotic.In Madhabkunda you will see the great waterfall – falls of million tons of water form 200ft. height. Big bolder of stones and the black stones in giving a shape of care in Madhabkundu.


Lawacherra Rain Forest

Lawacherra Rain Forest is one of the important & well-reserved forests in Bangladesh. Here visitor may see gibbons swimming through the trees and birds like bee-eater owls parrot. It is a good habitant of Deer, leopard, wild chicken, squirrel, and python.


Jaflong

Jaflong is one of the most attractive tourist spots in Sylhet division. It is situated besides the river Mari in the lap of Hill Khashia. Jaflong is a scenic spot nearby amidst tea gardens and rate beauty of rolling stones from hills.
The other tourist areas nearby Jaflong are Tamabil, Sripur and Jaintapur.
